Senior Software Engineer Madrid, Spain

About Magnolia:

At Magnolia, we empower our clients to manage their virtual presence. Flexible. Scalable. Mobile. Magnolia CMS' mobile-inspired user interface and customizable app framework have revolutionized the world of content management. Join us to strengthen Magnolia's position as the most user-friendly enterprise CMS.

What we are looking for:

We are looking for an experienced Java Engineer with a passion for solving problems as part of an agile team.

If you strive to create value in innovative ways and instead of trying to "think outside of the box" you simply get rid of it - this is when you're the perfect match for us.

Are we the right match for you? Please read on.

What you will do:

  • Develop new features for Magnolia and assure they comply with our quality standards.
  • Maintain the core content management functionality: from the user interface to rendered content including the template system, resources, and integration with front-end technologies.
  • Take the technical lead of new initiatives and draft architecture and design concepts for larger changes.
  • Facilitate collaboration with other engineers, product owner, and designers to solve interesting and challenging problems.
  • Deal with and improve dependency management, source control, issue tracking tools, and testing frameworks we use.
  • Help to spread new ideas and proven practices like TDD, Clean Code, Scrum, DevOps.

Required qualifications:

  • BS in Computer Science or equivalent
  • Smart, knowledgeable, curious and enthusiastic people
  • Minimum of 5 years Java development experience, Enterprise Java development is a plus
  • Advanced experience with web technologies
  • Experience with dependency management, source version control, issue tracking tools, and testing frameworks (for example Maven, Git, Jira, JUnit/Selenium)
  • Ability to work within an agile team
  • Experience with Vaadin or GWT is a bonus
  • Fluent English is required, additional languages are a plus

How we work:

We’re passionate about what we create; design and craft matter to us - whether it be in the graphics, the user experience or the code. Creating open source software that everyone can dig into keeps us honest & our code clean. We use state-of-the-art development tools and practices such as Maven, continuous integration with Jenkins, automated UI testing with Selenium & Jira for issues and project management.

How Magnolia empowers your success:

  • A Great Product: Loved by clients and respected by analysts, Magnolia is one of the best CMS solutions on the market.
  • Directly Contribute to Magnolia’s Success: We value each individuals’ input and create opportunities to realize them.
  • Great variety in your areas of responsibility: You will be able to dive into any skill area within the company that allows you to grow according to your personal career goals.
  • International Exposure: With offices in the USA, Spain, Switzerland, Czech Republic, Singapore, and Vietnam you will collaborate with colleagues from over 30 Nationalities.

If this sounds like a role you see yourself in and believe you can contribute to Magnolia CMS become even more successful, apply now!

AWS Engineer Madrid, Spain

Sobre nosotros:

Somos una compañía de telecomunicaciones, digital, multinacional, multiservicio y multimarca, centrada en sus clientes e impulsada por dos motores: el Software y los Datos. Tenemos 264 millones de clientes en el mundo y estamos presentes en 27 países. En España, contamos con 20,3 millones de clientes (31/12/18), tanto particulares como empresas.

Impulsamos proyectos innovadores, nuevos productos y negocios, como Big Data, Internet of Things, 5G, Ciberseguridad, virtualización, etc.

Sobre el trabajo:

El Ingeniero de Soporte Cloud tiene como responsabilidad el correcto funcionamiento de la cloud de Orange. Debe velar porque los servicios están disponibles un 99,99% y que su rendimiento sea el óptimo tanto a nivel de Computo, Almacenamiento, comunicaciones, etc. En este sentido se encargará de realizar todas las tareas de mantenimiento y medición de la infraestructura Cloud para mantenerla a punto, así como atender posibles incidentes que ocurran en el servicio, buscando el restablecimiento del mismo tan pronto sea posible.

Además, debe construir soluciones de capacidad y participar en procesos de capacidad de la infraestructura. Deberá llevar un control de las acciones a realizar para que los incidentes no vuelvan a generarse (gestión de problemas) y gestión transversal con otros grupos para buscar soluciones definitivas de los incidentes.

Es una posición que realizará soporte en modo 24x7 a turnos.

Perfil:

Profesional con al menos 5-10 años de experiencia en el sector de las Tecnologías de la Información y con experiencia demostrada de al menos 3 años como ingeniero de Soporte Cloud AWS. Se valorarán certificaciones en AWS.

Conocimientos requeridos:

  • Entendimiento profundo de la plataforma Cloud AWS
  • Entendimiento profundo de las implicaciones de Seguridad en Cloud.
  • Lenguajes para Cloud: Python, Ansible, Perl, Chef, Puppet.
  • Herramientas Cloud: Terraform, Rancher, Kubernetes, Openshift, Cloud Foundry.
  • Herramientas DevOps (CI/CD): Jenkins, GitLab, Sonar, Nexus, Jira, Confluence, etc.
  • SSOO: Microsoft, Linux Redhat.
  • Redes: switching/routing, DNS, LB, etc.
  • Middleware: Apache, Weblogic, Tomcat.
  • BBDD Relacionales: Oracle, Postgres, María DB, etc.
  • Herramientas de Monitorización: Prometheus, Sonar, etc.
  • Y adicionalmente: conocimiento en metodologías Agile, DevOps.

Envíanos tu CV a talento.es@orange.com o inscríbete en la oferta.
